Chaturya - Prakriti Questionnaire

What is Prakriti?

Prakriti is your unique Ayurvedic constitution, formed by the balance of three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. It is determined at birth and remains constant throughout life.

Why is Knowing Your Prakriti Important?

Understanding your Prakriti helps you choose the right diet, exercise, and lifestyle. It allows you to prevent diseases, maintain emotional balance, and age gracefully with personalized wellness.
